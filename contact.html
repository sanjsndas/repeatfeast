Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a recipe to RepeatFeast? +

We love receiving recipe submissions from our community! Please email your recipe along with photos to submissions@repeatfeast.com. Include your name, a brief description of the recipe, and any special notes about its origin or preparation. Our team reviews all submissions and will contact you if we decide to feature your recipe.

What's the best way to report a problem with the website? +

For technical issues, please email support@repeatfeast.com with details about the problem you're experiencing, including which browser and device you're using. Screenshots are very helpful! For content-related issues (incorrect recipes, inappropriate content), use the "Report" button on the relevant page or email content@repeatfeast.com.

How can I advertise on RepeatFeast? +

We offer several advertising opportunities for food-related brands and services. Please contact our partnerships team at ads@repeatfeast.com with information about your company and your target audience. We'll respond with our media kit and available advertising options.

Do you offer cooking classes or in-person events? +

We periodically host virtual cooking classes and occasionally in-person events in select cities. Sign up for our newsletter to receive announcements about upcoming events. If you're interested in hosting a RepeatFeast event in your area, email events@repeatfeast.com with your location and ideas.

How do I manage my newsletter subscription? +

You can update your newsletter preferences at any time by clicking the "Manage Preferences" link at the bottom of any newsletter email. To unsubscribe, click the "Unsubscribe" link. If you're having trouble, email support@repeatfeast.com and we'll help you update your settings.
